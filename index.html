What it does

Everything a school needs,
one app away

A complete platform for transparent, community-driven school development.

📋

Needs Dashboard

Headmasters list micro-needs with cost estimates and priority levels. Alumni see exactly what help is needed.

📊

Progress Tracking

Visual progress bars show how much has been pledged for each need in real time. Nothing is hidden.

🤝

Pledge System

Alumni pledge support with a single tap. Commitments are recorded instantly — no real money involved.

🏆

Hall of Fame

A ranked leaderboard celebrating alumni who contribute the most. Gold, silver, and bronze for top contributors.

📸

Impact Photos

Before and after photos prove completed work. Builds trust and shows exactly where support went.

🔐

Role-Based Access

Admins manage needs. Alumni view and pledge. Secure Firebase Authentication keeps data safe.

User flow

Simple. Transparent.
Impactful.

From listing a need to completing it — the whole journey in four steps.

01

Headmaster lists a need

Admin logs in and adds a micro-need with title, description, cost estimate, and priority level.

02

Alumni browse needs

Alumni open the app, see all current school needs with progress bars showing how much support is still needed.

03

Alumni pledge support

One tap on "Pledge Support" records the commitment. Progress bar updates. Name appears in Hall of Fame.

04

Work completed, photos shared

After the need is fulfilled, before/after photos are uploaded proving the impact to the entire community.
